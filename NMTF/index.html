WHO WE ARE

OUR VISION:

To impact and support the less privelged by chnaging their status and ensuring a lasting transformation.

OUR MISSION:

Meeting the basic needs of the less priveleged women and children by providing meaningful and sustainable support.

OBJECTIVES:

  1. To initiate and provide support to the needy.
  2. To provide basic health care and good nutrition.
  3. To educate and rehabilitate the less privelege children by providing scholarship programs.
  4. To provide life skills through vocational trainings.
  5. To organise counselling sessions through conferences nd seminars on matters relating to drug abuse, rape child abuse and sexual and gender based violence.
